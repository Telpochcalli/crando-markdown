### The History of Pizza...

### Ingredients:
- 2 cups bread flour
- 1 cup water
- 1 oz active yeast
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 tsp salt
 
- Tomato sauce 
- Cheese
> Notice how there is **no** pinneaple required.
